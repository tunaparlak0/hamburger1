ABOUT US

In the heart of Houston, amidst the hustle and bustle of the city, lies a hidden gem known as Smash Burgers. This quaint diner isn't just any burger joint; it's a testament to friendship, passion, and culinary artistry.


It all began over three decades ago when Jake and Mike, two childhood friends with a shared love for good food, embarked on a journey to fulfill their dream of creating the perfect burger. With unwavering determination and a secret recipe passed down through generations, they set out to make their mark in the culinary world.


After years of experimentation and countless late-night grill sessions in their backyard, Jake and Mike finally perfected their craft – the Smash Burger. Made with handpicked ingredients, each patty is meticulously seasoned and pressed to perfection on a sizzling hot griddle, resulting in a juicy, flavorful masterpiece that keeps customers coming back for more.


Word of mouth spread like wildfire, and soon, Smash Burgers became a beloved fixture in the Houston food scene. From locals craving a taste of nostalgia to tourists seeking an authentic Texan experience, people from all walks of life flock to this humble diner to indulge in the simple pleasure of a delicious burger.


But beyond the mouthwatering food lies the heart and soul of Smash Burgers – the bond between Jake and Mike. Through the highs and lows of entrepreneurship, their friendship has remained steadfast, serving as the driving force behind their success.


As they stand side by side behind the counter, flipping burgers and sharing stories with customers, Jake and Mike couldn't be prouder of what they've accomplished together. For them, Smash Burgers isn't just a business; it's a labor of love, a testament to the power of friendship, and a reminder that dreams do come true – one burger at a time.
