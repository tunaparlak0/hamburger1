About Us

WHO WE ARE?

Our founders Tom & Phil started Smash from selling burgers in a tent at parties and festivals in 2010, while always having a goal and drive to deliver the freshest burgers and chips with heart-warming, old-school hospitality - we believe in that homely feel-good feeling. We’ve now got 45 restaurants across the USA and to champion the freshest, tastiest produce, our patties, chips, sauces and veggies are prepped in our very own central prep kitchen, by our team, so we can assure to deliver quality every time. We love our community, therefore every Smash restaurant serves a local burger in collaboration with neighborhood food heroes whose flavor combinations have typically been created by the restaurant teams themselves.
